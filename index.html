Poky Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Poky is a personal save-for-later app: you save links, notes, and screenshots, and Poky helps you find and re-see them. This page explains what data Poky handles and what happens to it.

Contact: churemy@gmail.com

What Poky collects

  • Account details. Your email address and a password. The password is stored in hashed form by our authentication provider (Supabase); we never see it in plain text.
  • The content you save. Links, notes, screenshots and images, plus the titles, tags, and categories attached to them. This content is private to your account.
  • Text extracted from screenshots. On iOS, text inside images you save is read directly on your device using Apple's built-in text recognition. The image itself is not sent anywhere for this.
  • Basic usage events. To understand whether Poky works, the app records a few small events in our own database — when the app is opened, when an item is saved (and how: manually or via the share sheet), when a search leads to opening a save, and when a review session is completed. These events contain no content of your saves, are tied to your account, are never shared or sold, and are deleted with your account.
  • Crash reports. If the app crashes, a technical report (device model, OS version, and what the app was doing at the moment of the crash) is sent to Sentry so we can find and fix the bug. Crash reports are not used for advertising or profiling.

Poky has no ads and no third-party advertising or marketing trackers, and we do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

How your content is stored

  • Your saves are stored in a database and file storage hosted by Supabase. Each account's data is isolated with row-level security.
  • Images live in a private storage bucket under a path tied to your account, and are displayed only through short-lived signed links. They are not publicly accessible.

How your content is processed

  • Link previews. When you save a link, our server fetches that page to pick up its title, description, and preview information.
  • AI enrichment. After you save an item, a short excerpt of its text (your note text, link metadata, or text extracted from a screenshot on your device) is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a short summary, a few tags, and a category. The result is stored once so the same item is not processed repeatedly. Your images are never sent to AI providers — only extracted text is.

AI processing never runs while you are typing or searching; it only runs after an item is saved.

Service providers

Poky shares data only with the providers needed to run the app:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, file storage, and the serverless functions that process saves. Usage events are stored here too.
  • Google (Gemini API) — AI text processing described above.
  • Sentry — crash reporting described above.

Your data, your control

  • Delete a save any time from the item screen.
  • Delete your account in Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your saves, your stored images, and your account itself.
  • Reset your password from the sign-in screen with an emailed code.

Your data is kept until you delete it. There is no backup retention beyond the standard short-term backups of our hosting provider.

Children

Poky is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.
